Project Overview

Workgen Solutions partnered with Topgolf to deliver structured, end-to-end backend support for its high-volume hospitality and entertainment operations.

The objective was to streamline reservation management, event coordination, billing processes and guest support workflows while ensuring alignment with Topgolf's premium, guest-centric service standards.

Operating as an extension of the client's internal teams, Workgen managed critical backend functions that enabled venue teams to focus on delivering high-energy, on-site guest experiences while backend operations were executed with accuracy, speed and consistency.

Operational Environment

Fast-Paced Environment

Real-time coordination between backend systems and on-ground teams was critical to seamless guest operations.

SevenRooms CRM

All guest interactions and reservations were managed through SevenRooms with structured workflows for communication and billing.

Multi-Stakeholder Coordination

Seamless coordination across guests, venue staff, and internal departments with high accuracy and rapid turnaround.

Key Challenges

High-Volume Operations

Handling large volumes of reservation modifications, cancellations, and event bookings with accuracy and speed, without impacting guest experience.

Billing Accuracy & Refunds

Ensuring billing accuracy and minimizing refund-related discrepancies across a high volume of financial transactions under tight timelines.

Time-Sensitive Guest Management

Managing guest expectations in time-sensitive scenarios — last-minute changes, large group requests, and urgent escalations — without disruption.

Multi-Team Coordination

Maintaining consistent communication standards while coordinating effectively with multiple venue teams, stakeholders, and internal departments.

Our Approach

Workgen implemented a structured backend operations framework across five core functional pillars, each designed for efficiency, accuracy, and guest satisfaction.

End-to-End Event Management

  • Managed full lifecycle — creation, modification and cancellations
  • Escalated complex or urgent requests to venue teams
  • Ensured timely confirmations through standardized workflows

Reservation Management

  • Booking updates, cancellations & guest profiles via SevenRooms
  • Maintained audit trails for all changes ensuring accountability
  • Confirmation workflows including reminders to reduce no-shows

Billing & Refund Operations

  • Validated transactions and investigated billing discrepancies
  • Processed refunds in compliance with company policies
  • Maintained detailed logs for financial tracking and audit purposes

Guest Experience & Escalations

  • Managed complaints and service recovery with structured workflows
  • Lost & found tracking and coordination
  • Routed high-priority issues to relevant stakeholders immediately

Internal & Associate Support

  • Handled onboarding queries and operational requests
  • Internal coordination for scheduling, policies and support needs
  • Centralized tracking of all requests for visibility and follow-up

Operational Workflow

Every request — whether from a guest or internal team — followed a structured five-step process ensuring accuracy, accountability, and timely resolution.

1

Request Intake

Guest and internal requests were received via CRM systems, email workflows, and support channels, ensuring no request was missed or delayed.

2

Validation & Processing

Requests were reviewed for accuracy, eligibility, and policy compliance before execution — preventing errors from reaching downstream systems.

3

System Updates

All changes were processed and updated within SevenRooms with complete audit trails, maintaining full accountability for every action taken.

4

Communication & Coordination

Guests and internal teams were informed through standardized communication templates and workflows, ensuring clarity and consistency in every interaction.

5

Escalation & Resolution

Complex or high-priority cases were escalated to venue leadership and tracked through to full resolution with complete documentation.

Program Management
