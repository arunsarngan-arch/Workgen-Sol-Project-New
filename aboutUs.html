Our Journey

How We Got Here

A story of perseverance, precision, and a passion for data.

2017 — The Beginning

Workgen Solutions is Founded

Starting from a small office in Noida, we began with a core team focused on back-office operations and data entry, driven by a belief that quality outsourcing could transform businesses of any size.

2018–2019 — Expanding Services

Data Annotation & QA/QC Added

As AI and ML gained momentum, we expanded into data annotation and quality monitoring — positioning Workgen as a full-service data solutions provider ready for the next wave of technology.

2020–2021 — Going Global

International Client Reach

Breaking geographical barriers, we began serving clients across North America, Europe, and Southeast Asia — proving that quality BPO from India could compete on the world stage.

2022–Present — ML & AI Focus

ML Annotation & Advanced Services

With the AI boom accelerating, we launched specialized ML annotation services covering image, text, audio and video datasets — becoming a trusted partner for AI-first companies worldwide.
