Project Overview

Workgen Solutions partnered with Topgolf to deliver a high-volume call auditing and quality assurance program for its customer service operations. The objective was to ensure that every guest interaction reflected Topgolf's fun, welcoming, and premium hospitality experience.

Our QA team systematically reviewed inbound and outbound guest calls, ensuring that agents provided accurate information about bookings, events, pricing, and venue policies while maintaining high standards of professionalism and guest engagement.

Through structured evaluation, performance monitoring, and coaching feedback, the program helped strengthen customer service quality and improve overall guest satisfaction.

Key Challenges

High Call Volume

Topgolf's rapidly growing customer base created a high call volume environment that required a scalable QA solution.

Consistency at Scale

Maintaining consistent service quality across multiple agents while ensuring accurate information about reservations, pricing, and events.

Communication Gaps

Identifying communication gaps affecting guest experience without impacting agent morale or efficiency.

Performance Monitoring

Monitoring agent performance at scale while maintaining audit accuracy and operational efficiency.

Our Approach

Workgen implemented a structured QA framework designed to monitor and improve customer interactions across the entire contact center.

📊

High-Volume Audit Program

  • 300–400 call audits completed weekly
  • Minimum 1 audit per agent every week
  • 40 calls audited per QA analyst per day
✓

Standardized Evaluation

  • FCC Master Quality Form scorecard
  • Scored on accuracy, professionalism, empathy
  • Resolution quality metrics tracked
💡

Coaching-Driven Improvement

  • Detailed coaching notes for each call
  • Communication gap identification
  • Continuous feedback loops for improvement

Call Auditing Process

1

Call Recording

Customer interactions were recorded through the Five9 platform, ensuring every conversation could be reviewed accurately.

2

Call Evaluation

QA analysts accessed recordings and evaluated calls using structured scorecards aligned with Topgolf's service standards.

3

Quality Scoring

Each call was scored based on: accuracy of information, communication clarity, professionalism, customer engagement, and issue resolution quality.

4

Coaching Feedback

Detailed notes were provided highlighting agent strengths, communication errors, and specific opportunities for improvement with actionable recommendations.

5

Performance Monitoring

Results were aggregated into dashboards and reports to track agent performance trends and real-time service quality metrics.

Program Management

To maintain consistency and operational alignment, Workgen implemented structured management practices:

📋

Weekly Refresh

Audit lists refreshed weekly to ensure complete agent coverage and balanced workload distribution.

📊

Daily Monitoring

Auditor productivity and QA output monitored daily to maintain operational efficiency.

🎯

Calibration Sessions

Regular calibration sessions held to maintain scoring consistency across all QA analysts.

🤝

Structured Reporting

Regular reporting and collaboration with client stakeholders for transparent communication.
