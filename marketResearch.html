Project Overview

Workgen Solutions delivers comprehensive, end-to-end market research and mystery shopping operations for global consumer brands, retail chains, and technology companies — providing high-accuracy, actionable insights that enhance brand consistency and optimise customer journeys.

Our operations cover physical stores, online platforms, and hybrid channels, evaluating the complete customer experience across in-store visits, digital touchpoints, chatbot interactions, and telephonic surveys.

Through structured audit frameworks, multi-layer quality validation, and technology-enabled reporting, the program provides clients with consistent, comparable data to drive measurable improvement in CSAT, NPS, and first-contact resolution rates.

Key Challenges

Consistency at Scale

Ensuring consistent audit quality across multiple locations, channels, and distributed auditors simultaneously.

Data Accuracy & Validation

Maintaining high data accuracy and validation standards across all audit channels and submission types.

Omni-Channel Monitoring

Monitoring both physical and digital customer experiences simultaneously across diverse, distributed touchpoints.

Journey Gap Identification

Identifying customer journey gaps across diverse, distributed touchpoints without disrupting normal operations.

Distributed Partner Coordination

Efficiently coordinating a distributed network of auditors and field partners while managing onboarding, communication, and incentive payments at scale.

Workgen implemented a multi-channel audit framework with standardised evaluation tools, robust QA validation, and end-to-end partner management to ensure operational excellence at every stage.

Audit Process

1

Audit Assignment

Auditors assigned based on location, channel, and project requirements for optimal geographic and operational coverage.

2

Mystery Shopping Execution

In-store visits, online evaluations, chatbot testing, and telephonic surveys conducted per pre-defined scenarios and evaluation criteria.

3

Data Collection & Submission

Detailed observations recorded covering service quality, brand compliance, merchandising accuracy, and overall customer experience.

4

Quality Validation

Multi-level QA checks validate accuracy, consistency, and completeness of all submitted audit data before delivery to the client.

5

Reporting & Insights

Validated data aggregated into structured reports and dashboards highlighting trends, performance gaps, and actionable improvement opportunities.
