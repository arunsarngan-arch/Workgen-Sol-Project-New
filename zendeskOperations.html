Project Overview

Workgen Solutions provides end-to-end centralized support and escalation management powered by Zendesk for enterprise clients across retail, field service, and multi-location operations.

The objective is to streamline fragmented support processes into a unified ticketing system, ensuring faster issue resolution, strict SLA adherence, and seamless coordination across all stakeholders.

Acting as an extension of client teams, Workgen manages complex queries, operational issues, and partner escalations — ensuring business continuity and enhanced service efficiency at every level.

Key Challenges

Fragmented Communication Channels

Fragmented communication channels across multiple stakeholders leading to delays, inefficiencies, and missed resolution windows.

High-Volume Escalations

High volume of store-level and partner escalations demanding rapid, structured responses to prevent operational disruptions.

SLA Compliance Across Categories

Maintaining SLA compliance across diverse ticket categories while managing varying priority levels and resolution timelines simultaneously.

Consistent Communication Standards

Ensuring consistent and professional communication across all ticket categories and stakeholder touchpoints without service degradation.

Recurring Issue Escalation Overload

Identifying recurring issues and preventing escalation overload — without centralized systems, delays and lack of visibility directly impact operations and stakeholder satisfaction.

Our Approach

Workgen implemented a structured Zendesk-based support framework focused on efficiency, transparency, and scalability — ensuring consistent service delivery and full operational control at every stage.

🎫

Centralized Ticketing System

  • All queries, issues, and escalations managed through Zendesk
  • Unified platform for end-to-end tracking and resolution
  • Full visibility across all open, pending, and resolved tickets
⏱️

SLA-Driven Operations

  • Defined SLAs for response and resolution times per ticket category
  • Priority-based ticket routing and escalation handling
  • Automated alerts for aging or high-priority cases
🔗

Cross-Functional Coordination

  • Collaboration between stores, field teams, finance, and operations
  • Alignment across stakeholders for timely and accurate resolution
  • Standardized communication protocols for consistent responses
📈

Insight-Driven Continuous Improvement

  • Root-cause analysis of recurring issues to prevent recurrence
  • Data-driven recommendations to reduce future escalations
  • Regular performance reporting to stakeholders with actionable insights

Support & Escalation Process

1

Ticket Creation

Issues are logged via email or directly into Zendesk, capturing all relevant details including category, priority, and stakeholder context.

2

Ticket Categorization & Routing

Tickets are categorized based on priority and assigned to appropriate teams for resolution using predefined routing logic.

3

Investigation & Resolution

Teams coordinate across functions to resolve issues efficiently while maintaining clear, professional communication with all parties involved.

4

SLA Monitoring

Tickets are continuously tracked against SLAs with automated alerts for aging cases or high-priority escalations requiring immediate attention.

5

Closure & Documentation

Resolved tickets are documented with detailed notes, ensuring full transparency, audit readiness, and a knowledge base for recurring issue prevention.
